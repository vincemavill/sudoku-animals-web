# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Animals

**Last Updated: June 11, 2026**

Sudoku Animals ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy, especially since our app is designed for users of all ages, including children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
Sudoku Animals collects a **Display Name** and **Profile Icon** chosen by you to personalize your gaming experience. We do not collect your real name, email address, or phone number. No account creation is required.

### 1.2 Gameplay Data
Your gameplay progress, including your current level, unlocked animal stickers, "Mastery Mode" achievements, and saved games, is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. No gameplay data is synced, sent, or uploaded to any remote or cloud databases.

Because your gameplay data is stored exclusively on your device, it will not be shared or uploaded to the cloud. Note that if you uninstall the app or clear its data, your progress will be permanently reset.

### 1.3 Technical and Usage Data
When you use our app, we or our third-party partners (such as Firebase) may collect certain non-personal information automatically, including:
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFA for iOS or AAID for Android)
- General usage statistics and crash reports

This data is used solely to improve app performance and understand how users interact with our features.

## 2. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions
Our app offers premium features, such as "Remove Ads" and "Unlimited Hints," through weekly, monthly, or yearly subscriptions. 
- **Payment Processing:** All financial transactions are handled by the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play Store**. We do not collect or store your credit card numbers or billing information.
- **Subscription Management:** We may store an anonymized transaction ID to verify your premium status and restore purchases across your devices.

## 3. Advertising
To keep the basic version of Sudoku Animals free, we may display third-party advertisements.
- These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests.
- You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device's system settings.
- We strive to ensure that ads displayed are appropriate for all ages.

## 4. Permissions and Media Sharing
Our app includes features that allow you to share your progress and rewards:
- **Photo Library / Storage Access:** We request permission to save "Animal Sticker" rewards and "Completion Certificates" to your device's gallery. We only access your storage to write these specific files at your request.
- **Sharing Features:** When you use the "Share" button to send a certificate or sticker to friends, this is handled through your device's native sharing interface. We do not track who you share with or the content of your messages.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use professional third-party services to enhance your experience:
- **Firebase:** For anonymized analytics and performance monitoring.
- **Expo Framework:** To power the app's cross-platform functionality.
- **Ad Networks:** To provide advertisements (in the free version).

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Sudoku Animals is a family-friendly app. We comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)** and similar global regulations.
- We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
- If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so we can delete it immediately.

## 7. Data Retention
Since all data is stored locally, it is permanently deleted if you uninstall the app. If you use the "Reset Game" or "Start Fresh" features within the app, your local progress will be reset.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## 9. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact the developer directly at **vincemavill@gmail.com** or through the official App Store or Google Play Store listing support channels.
